Our Story

Born in the
Himalayas,
Served in Indore

Khau Nepali Momos was born from a simple, irresistible idea — to bring the soulful, authentic taste of Nepal to the streets of Indore. Every momo we make is a tribute to the rich culinary heritage of the Himalayas.

Nestled in Bengali Square, we've built a home where every guest is family. Our dough is hand-kneaded fresh every morning. Our fillings are crafted from recipes passed down through generations. And our chutneys? Fiery, tangy, and utterly addictive.

Whether you're here for a quick bite or a long evening with friends, Khau promises the same thing every time — warmth, flavour, and momos that make you come back for more.
